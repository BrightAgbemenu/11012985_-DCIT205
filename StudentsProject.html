Library Management System

Library Management System

Libraries are increasingly using computers to manage their collections and circulation. As a result, Library Management Systems (LMS) have become an important tool for the library staff. The LMS is designed to help the library track and manage their books, e-books, journals, and other materials. They can also be used to manage patron information and circulation records.
