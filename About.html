Welcome

Welcome to the Information Technology page, where Computer Science stands at its core. The study of computer science is indispensable for society to fully leverage the benefits that Information Technology offers, propelling rapid economic advancements. Computer science delves into the study and development of applications facilitating our everyday use of computers, the supportive software systems, and the methodologies employed to create applications and systems that operate reliably, correctly, and ethically. At the heart of Computer Science lies the proficiency in computer programming. Employers seeking Computer Scientists anticipate adept programmers with knowledge of contemporary techniques and tools, possessing the ability to apply sound engineering principles to the development, construction, and maintenance of computer programs. Explore the dynamic world of Computer Science, where innovation meets practical application.

The array of courses offered in the department equips students with a versatile skill set, paving the way for employment across a spectrum of economic sectors. Our graduates are well-prepared for the dynamic landscape of the computing industry, where they can contribute to project teams or engage in research and development endeavors. The curriculum is designed to empower graduates to bring specialized skills to their respective teams. Moreover, the course serves as a launching pad for further study or research, offering exposure to both established and emerging technologies and methods, as well as active fields of research within the realm of Computer Science. Explore a world of opportunities and continual learning through our comprehensive program.

TThe Department presents two distinctive undergraduate degree programs: the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with options for Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) structures, alongside the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. Explore the diverse pathways within these programs, each tailored to provide a comprehensive and specialized educational experience.

Our Organization

The University of Ghana is one of the Africa's most preeminent public universities. Our impact on individuals, our region and the world is profound. Whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting the grand challenges of our time through dauntless research. UG educates more than 38,000students annually, with approximately 2.64% being computer science students. We turn ideas into impact and transform the world around us.
